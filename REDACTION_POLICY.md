# Redaction Policy

This repository is a public exploratory research surface. It is designed to show toy quantum and AI-adjacent experimentation without exposing private systems, proprietary architecture, internal receipts, private research logs, or operational details.

## Allowed Public Content

The repository may include:

- Toy circuits
- Synthetic data
- Simulator outputs
- Public methodology notes
- Clearly labeled exploratory results
- Reproducible educational examples
- Redacted hardware notes
- Limitation-focused analysis

## Prohibited Content

Do not commit:

- Private production architecture
- Real private system receipts
- Proprietary schemas
- Internal codenames or governance labels
- Private research logs that reveal non-public systems
- API tokens, provider credentials, `.env` files, or account identifiers
- Customer, partner, employee, or personal data
- Claims of speedup or production relevance without reproducible evidence

## Review Rule

Before adding any artifact, ask:

1. Is this reproducible by an outside reviewer?
2. Is this clearly labeled as toy, exploratory, or independently verified?
3. Does it expose any private system or operational method?
4. Could the same idea be shown using a safer synthetic example?

When in doubt, publish the synthetic example and keep the private artifact out of the repository.
